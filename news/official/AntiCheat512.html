Anti-Cheat Enforcement Update for PvP Gameplay

2026-05-12 LATEST

Dear Wanderers,

 

Our core mission is to build and maintain a fair, just, and healthy competitive environment. We take fair play extremely seriously and are constantly monitoring and acting against violations. Recently, we have identified instances of players using third-party cheating tools, including aimbots and scripts, to gain unfair advantages in PvP gameplay. This behavior severely undermines the principles of fair play and has a toxic effect on the game's environment and the experience of all legitimate players.

 

Effective immediately, we are launching a comprehensive crackdown on cheating in PvP modes. We will be upgrading our security detection systems and strengthening our manual review processes. Any account confirmed to be in violation will be subject to the strictest penalties available, up to and including permanent account bans and device-level login restrictions, with no exceptions.

 

We rely on the community to help identify and report suspicious behavior. If you encounter a player you suspect of cheating during a match, please submit a report through the official in-game feedback channel.

 

How to report:

In-game Home Menu > Settings > Contact Support > Report a Player

 

To help our security team investigate efficiently, please include the following information with your report:

[Reported Player's ID]:

[Reported Player's Character Name]:

[Game Mode]: (Please specify, e.g., 1v1 Arena, Group Strategy, etc.)

[Time of Incident]: (Please be as specific as possible, e.g., Month/Day, Hour:Minute)

[Description of Behavior]: (Please provide an objective description of the suspicious activity, e.g., abnormal movement speed, automated Deflection, etc.)

[Video/Image Link]: (This is critical evidence. Please provide a link to a recording or screenshot that captures the suspicious behavior.)

 

Every report submitted will be thoroughly reviewed by our security team in accordance with standard procedures. All confirmed violations will be acted upon. We also guarantee that the personal information and report history of all individuals who submit reports will be kept strictly confidential.

 

Fair competition is the foundation of this game's long-term health. We sincerely appreciate your continued support, and we look forward to working together with all Wanderers to protect the integrity of our game.
