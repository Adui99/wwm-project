Redefine Everything—Where Winds Meet Global Launch

2025-11-14 LATEST

One question. What is wuxia to you?

Poetry of impossible combat and dances through air? That burning dedication of a lifetime spent perfecting a single strike? Or the quiet heroism of doing what's right when no one's watching?
Everyone has their own answer, and that's their own vision of the jianghu. Where Winds Meet is vast enough for all of them.

Forge Your Own Path

This is a living, breathing world without boundaries. Scale mountains that pierce the clouds. Dive into valleys shrouded in mist. The choice is yours: dedicate yourself to a single martial sect and master its secrets, or walk alone and learn in secret from every stranger to forge something entirely new. Maybe you'll put down your sword altogether. Become a silver-tongued diplomat who shapes kingdoms with words. Or a wandering healer whose skills bring hope to the desperate.
Journey solo when you need solitude, and feel the pulse of the world in perfect stillness. Call upon friends when the challenge demands it, and take on legendary foes on the path of trials. Test your skills against ther players in the arena, then explore the vast world together to create stories no developer could script.
Version 1.0 brings you a main storyline that will move you to tears and laughter in equal measure, alongside countless side quests, hidden encounters, and boss battles that will test everything you've learned.

Lose Yourself in a Living World

Time flows here as it should. Dawn breaks gently over mountain peaks. Storms gather on the horizon. Ride through bamboo forests as leaves whisper overhead. Stand alone in the wilderness, eyes closed, listening to the wind. Watch stars wheel overhead and know that this moment, this choice, is yours alone.
Every soul in our cities and villages lives their own life. Even stray cats have their routines. These aren't decorations, but people with stories, fears, and dreams. A casual conversation might reveal crucial intelligence. The right words might convince someone to change their entire life. Your actions ripple outward in ways you won't always predict.

Master the Art of Combat

Every weapon tells multiple stories. A simple blade becomes a whirlwind of steel in one warrior's hands, a precise surgical instrument in another's. Layer martial arts with internal arts and mystic arts. Make the legendary real: Tai Chi that genuinely redirects force, Lion's Roar that shakes mountains, acupoint strikes that paralyze instantly. Each boss demands its own strategy, its own dance of death and survival. We've obsessed over every frame of combat to deliver something that feels powerful, fluid, and uniquely yours.

Your Legend, Your Way

Where Winds Meet doesn't gate content behind paywalls or force you down predetermined paths. PvP, PvE, casual activities... everything is free and everything rewards progression materials. Play what you love, ignore what you don't. The rewards follow your choices, not the other way around.
Our monetization focuses on appearances, battle passes, and monthly passes. Let us be crystal clear: we have no P2W here. Your legend is written by skill and choice, never by wallet size. We will never sell power. We will never compromise the integrity of combat and gameplay for profit. This is our promise.

Years of development have brought us here, but we know this is just the beginning. Where Winds Meet isn't perfect, because no living world ever is. We'll stumble sometimes. But we're here for the long haul, ready to listen, ready to evolve. This isn't just our game anymore. It's yours.

Welcome to the jianghu. Your legend starts now.
