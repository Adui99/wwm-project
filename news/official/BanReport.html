Where Winds Meet - Account Suspension for Violations

2025-12-06 LATEST

Dear Wanderers,

Since the official launch, Eastwind Pavilion has been actively monitoring the use of third-party assists. In addition to thoroughly validating reports, we have implemented technical measures to better detect abnormal in-game behaviors. 

Accounts confirmed to be in violation will face strict penalties. We appreciate your understanding and support in making Jianghu a fair and just place.

Violations include, but are not limited to: cheating, speed hacks, macro tools, or any other software that disrupts fair play. 

Punishments will be determined on a case-by-case basis. In gameplay scenarios like Arena, penalties may include removal from the leaderboard, prohibition from participating in certain game modes, and temporary or permanent account bans.

We strive to ensure a fair gaming experience while protecting your in-game assets. As we work to enhance game quality, we encourage all Wanderers to help us maintain a just environment. 
If you encounter any misconduct in-game, please report it using the in-game reporting feature. We take each case seriously and will continue to optimize the feedback system at Eastwind Pavilion.

For any questions or requests, please reach out to us at our customer service email:
wherewindsmeet@global.netease.com.
