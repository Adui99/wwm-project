Version 1.7 Path Balance Adjustment Announcement (May 28)

2026-05-26 LATEST

Dear Wanderers:

Based on real-time data and feedback, this balance optimization focuses on adjusting Paths that underperformed or had room for improvement, while ensuring overall stability. We have also addressed core pain points frequently reported by Wanderers that significantly affected the experience, and made targeted adjustments to the experience and balance of certain game modes.

We welcome you to continue providing valuable suggestions. We will carefully listen to every piece of feedback and continue to make prudent adjustments to progression, Paths, and competitive modes in future versions to optimize experience.

 

I. Path Experience Optimization and Adjustments:

Bellstrike - Splendor:

1. For the Martial Art "Nameless Spear", the application timing of the "Endless Gale" effect from the Martial Art Skill "Qiankun's Lock" has been adjusted to the end of the skill cast. This prevents the effect from being wasted during the cast duration and aligns with the application timing of the "Jadeware" set.

2. The Martial Art Skill "Qiankun's Lock" in the Martial Art "Nameless Spear" now inflicts "Immobilized" when hitting non-Boss targets. When hitting Boss targets, it inflicts the "Qi Imbalance" state, increasing the Qi Damage taken by the target for its duration. This compensates for the inability to immobilize Bosses and strengthens the Path's characteristics.

3. For the Martial Art "Nameless Sword", the effective targets of the talent "Sword Energy Affinity Enhancement" have been adjusted. It is now effective against low Qi targets and targets in the "Qi Imbalance" state.

4. For the Martial Art "Nameless Sword", the talent "Qi Struggle Enhancement", which previously increased Qi Damage against all targets via Sword Energy attacks, has been adjusted. It now increases Qi Damage against player targets and increases HP Damage against non-player targets (up to a 20% increase). This better aligns the effect with different combat environments. Also fixed an issue where the extra Qi Damage scaling based on Max Physical Attack did not match the talent description.

5. For the Martial Art "Nameless Spear", added a new condition to the talent "Affinity DMG Boost": you can now also gain Affinity DMG Boost while under the "Endless Gale" effect.

6. Adjustments to the Inner Way "Sword Morph":

Basic Effect Adjustments: Reduced the forward movement distance when releasing multiple Sword Energies. Continuing to charge after completing Stage 2 Charge will no longer consume Endurance. The extra DMG Boost per point of Endurance consumed upon release is increased from 1% to 1.5%, and the max DMG Boost limit from consuming Endurance is raised to 30%.

Tier 1 and Tier 4 Effect Adjustments: As long as you start charging within 5 seconds after releasing the Special Skill "Shadow Step" and "Multiple Sword Energies", you can release "Multiple Sword Energies" upon completing Stage 2 Charge for the Charged Skill "Vagrant Sword". When the prerequisites for releasing "Multiple Sword Energies" are met, an indicator will appear on the status bar for easy tracking.

Tier 3 Effect Adjustments & Additions: "Multiple Sword Energies" hitting Exhausted targets will never result in an Abrasion, and the third Sword Energy hitting an Exhausted non-player target will guarantee an Affinity strike. The original Tier 3 "Sword Energy DMG Boost" effect is moved to the base Martial Art effect with an extended duration, and its benefits against non-player targets are increased.

7. Adjustments to the Inner Way "Mountain's Might":

Tier 1 Effect Adjustments: The original Tier 1 effect is merged into Tier 0. Added a new Tier 1 effect: Hitting a Boss target with any Martial Art Skill of the "Bellstrike - Splendor" Path will inflict the "Qi Imbalance" state.

Tier 6 Effect Adjustments: The Endurance recovery effect condition is adjusted so that the target must have "Moving Mountain" (inflicted on non-Bosses only) or be in "Qi Imbalance" (inflicted on Bosses only). This simplifies the number of effects Wanderers need to track across modes. Also adjusted the triggerable skill types, recovery values, and intervals for the Endurance recovery effect to make it more stable and better suited to the "Bellstrike - Splendor" Path.

8. Adjustments to the Inner Way "Battle Anthem": The cooldown for the Tier 3 effect is reduced to align with the cooldown of the "Bellstrike - Splendor" Path's Martial Art Skills and set effects. Tier 6 Effect Adjustments: The DMG Boost per 10 points of Endurance is increased from 1% to 2%, while the max limit remains unchanged at 10%.

9. Set "Jadeware": Effect duration is increased to 10 seconds to align with the duration of the "Endless Gale" effect, optimizing the set's activation experience.

10. Set "Jadeware": The effective condition for the extra buff is adjusted; it now takes effect against targets in the "Qi Imbalance" state.

 

Bellstrike - Umbra:

1. Martial Art "Heavenquaker Spear", talent "DoT Enhancement" effect adjustment: When the "Empowered Effect of DoT" settles, it now also grants an 18% Affinity DMG Boost, while the bonus granted upon DoT settlement is reduced accordingly. "Empowered Effect of DoT" includes "High Bleeding" and "Piercing Damage" from the Inner Way "Fivefold Bleed".

2. Inner Way "Wolfchaser's Art" Tier 4 effect addition: Using the Special Skill "Sweep All" during the "Empowered River Flow" effect will inflict an additional instance of "High Bleeding" (without clearing Bleeding stacks), with a 12-second effective interval.

3. Inner Way "Insightful Strike" effect adjustments:

Tier 1 Effect Adjustment: The active effect will now vary based on your Max HP ratio. When your HP is low, you still heal based on the DMG dealt; when your HP is high, you gain an extra 1.5% DMG Boost.

Tier 2 Effect Adjustment: "Bellstrike Attack Bonus" is changed to "Physical Attack Bonus".

Tier 3 Effect Adjustment: During the "Concentration" effect, the DMG Boost attribute is changed from 3% "Affinity Rate" to 1.5% "Direct Affinity Rate". Additionally, if your HP ratio is higher than the target's, you gain an extra 1.5% bonus.

Tier 5 Effect Adjustment: "Bellstrike Penetration Bonus" is changed to "Physical Penetration Bonus".

Tier 6 Effect Adjustment: During the "Concentration" effect, DMG from DoT and "Empowered Effect of DoT" is increased by 10%. This better suits the core strengths of the Bellstrike - Umbra and balances its DoT benefits.

4. Inner Way "Sword Horizon" Tier 3 effect adjustment: The guaranteed Affinity skill after fully stacking "Sword's Zenith" is changed from "Zenith Sword" to "High Bleeding". The Tier 6 effect description has been updated accordingly.

2. Martial Art "Vernal Umbrella": The Special Skill "Unfading Flower" deals 15% more DMG to non-player targets. Additionally, in non-Arena modes, ending the "Unfading Flower" state refunds 15 Blossoms, triggering up to once every 5 seconds.

Also adjusted the "Lingering Bone" refresh logic for the Special Skill "Unfading Flower": Enhanced ballistic hits will refresh all "Lingering Bone" marks on the target, including those applied by other Wanderers, regardless of the source.

5. Martial Art "Inkwell Fan": The Light Attack Charged Skill "Forsaken Fame" deals an extra 45% DMG and recovers Endurance when hitting non-player enemies. Hitting multiple targets only triggers the Endurance recovery once.

6. Martial Art "Inkwell Fan": The "Lingering Bone" mark applied by specific skills is now treated as an independent source and can no longer be shared.

7. Inner Way "Thunderous Bloom" effect adjustments:

Tier 0 Effect Adjustment: The trigger condition for gaining "Spring Thunder" is changed from moving a certain distance within a short time to finishing the cast of any Martial Art Skill. The skill types that trigger and benefit from this are changed from Heavy Attack/Airborne Heavy Attack/Light Attack/Light Attack Charge to Heavy Attack/Heavy Attack Pursuit/Light Attack/Ballistic Skill, keeping it consistent with the "Veil of the Willow" set. The DMG boost now applies to all aforementioned skill types instead of a single skill type.

Tier 1 Effect Adjustment: Removed the original effect. Added an effect that increases the number of "Spring Thunder" stacks gained per trigger.

Tier 3 Effect Adjustment: Removed the Qi Damage boost effect. Added an effect that extends the DMG boost duration per consumed "Spring Thunder" stack from 1 second to 2 seconds.

8. Fixed an issue where the exclusive "Blossoms" resource for the Martial Art "Vernal Umbrella" could be abnormally gained when dealing DoT.

9. Fixed an issue where the Martial Art "Vernal Umbrella" Special Skill "Unfading Flower" effect could sometimes briefly persist even when "Blossoms" were fully depleted.

10. Fixed an issue with the Inner Way "Star Reacher" where reapplying "Lingering Bone" to a target already marked with it failed to correctly trigger the related Inner Way effects.

11. Fixed an issue where attacking enemies affected by DoT with the Martial Art "Vernal Umbrella" would abnormally grant extra Blossoms.

 

Silkbind - Deluge:

1. Martial Art "Soulshade Umbrella" Martial Art Skill "Floating Grace" effect adjustment: The DMG Boost is increased to 24% when "Panacea Fan" is also equipped, and adjusted to 10% when not equipped.

2. Inner Way "Restoring Blossom" Tier 0, Tier 1, and Tier 3 adjustments: The duration of the "Nurturing" effect gained from Tier 0 is increased from 3 seconds to 6 seconds; the duration of the "Nurturing" effect gained from Tier 1 is increased from 6 seconds to 10 seconds; the duration of the "Concentration" effect gained from Tier 3 is increased from 6 seconds to 10 seconds. II. Arena Experience Optimization:

1. Endurance consumption adjustments: The max limit for Endurance consumption reduction effects is capped at 40%; the Endurance consumed after continuously Sprinting for over 1 second is increased; the reduction in Endurance recovery speed while players are in a Defense state is slightly decreased; drawing a bow (Charge) for over 1.2 seconds will start continuously consuming Endurance, and the charge stage will no longer increase once Endurance reaches 0.

2. Upon getting up after being Executed, players will gain brief Tenacity, Control Immunity, and Super Armor effects.

3. Players knocked down by an Execute will no longer take Qi Damage.

4. Reduced the terrain elevation differences within Arena mode maps to prevent them from affecting player combo performance.

5. Further adjusted the performance of Defense Counter skills for all weapons to level the differences in Defense Counter strength among different weapons.

6. Added a level adjustment for escaping the Hit state: The timing for Wanderers with different network latencies to escape a hit via Dodge, Deflect, or movement will remain exactly the same. Wanderers with high latency will no longer escape hits later than those with low latency.

7. When Wanderers move at high speeds in Arena modes, colliding with other Wanderers will cause a quick stop rather than clipping through each other.

8. Fixed an issue where Wanderers could trigger a Perfect Dodge during the brief Invincibility frame gained after a successful Deflect.

9. In Arena modes, adjusted the timing windows during which the post-cast animations of most Martial Art Skills can be interrupted by enemy player Light or Heavy Attacks, leveling the differences among various Martial Arts.

10. Optimized the hit detection logic of projectiles fired by high-latency opponents, making it easier to evade them via "Deflect", "Dodge", etc. Fixed an issue where players could still be hit by projectiles after successfully triggering a "Perfect Dodge".

11. After the Arena talent "Guarding Qi Core" is triggered, it no longer grants a 2-second "Damage Reduction" effect. Instead, it restores both HP and Qi simultaneously, clears control effects, and grants a 0.5-second "Invincibility" effect.

12. In the Arena mode, when a player triggers "Guarding Qi Core" without being in a hit stag state, the inherent crowd-control removal effect of "Guarding Qi Core" will not be triggered.

13. If a player triggers "Guarding Qi Core" while in an inescapable hit stag state, the invincibility effect of "Guarding Qi Core" will last until that hit stage state ends.

14. For a short period after being executed or after exiting hit stag, the progress bar for the passive crowd-control removal trigger will temporarily stop changing.

15. Fixed an issue where hitting enemies with certain "Guild War" Ultimates could interrupt some Tenacity effects.

16. Weapon "Sword": The cancel point of the first stage of "Heavy Attack" is moved earlier to stably chain into subsequent Heavy Attacks.

21. Martial Art "Nameless Spear": In Arena modes, the un-interruptible post-cast animation time (via "Deflect") is increased when the Martial Art Skill "Qiankun's Lock" misses the target.
